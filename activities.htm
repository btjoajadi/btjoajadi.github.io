Activities

White River Rafting

Bali has an abundance of places to go white river rafting, with ranging difficulties and many different routes throughout Bali's rivers. Perfect for family activities! Depending on the size of the party, visitors have the freedom to choose from more than 10 options to navigate the rivers of Bali, all in different parts of the island. Prices are seasonal and often negotiable, so make sure to bring a local!

Waterbom

Waterbom is a waterpark located near Kuta beach. It was rated as the best waterpark in Asia by trip advisor for the year 2017. Waterbom Bali spans over 3.5 hectares, and its customers can choose more than 15 activities to do, including a surf machine, a lazy river, and loads of fun and thrilling water slides. To allow its visitors to spend the whole day there, Waterbom has options for restaurants, bars, and lounge areas, making it the perfect destination for families.
Turtle Conservation at Serangan

Pet some turtles at the Turtle Conservation and Education Center at Serangan. A turtle lover's dream spot, the conservation center is located near sanur. One can volunteer at the center to help the conservation efforts in Bali Indonesia and aim to educate visitors on the dangers posed on the turtles of Bali. Turtle enthusiasts volunteers will also be able to rescue injured turtles and nurse them back to health before releasing them back into the wild.
Bali treetop

An obstacle course with varying difficulties placed above the trees, Bali Treetop is full of thrills for those who seek to challenge themselves in a "Ninja Warrior"-esque obstacle course. Children as young as 4 years old are eligible for some of the designated courses. Filled with swings, nets, and flying foxes, this obstacle course takes you between trees and over cliffs and makes sure you get your fair share of exercise for the day.
